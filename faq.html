About Us

We are team Llama in Pyjama, Daniele, Elena and Silvia, participating in Mongol Rally 2019 - an extreme road trip challenge starting from Central Europe all the way to Mongolia and ends at Russia this summer.

This summer we will have

1 small car with 3 Llamas in Pyjamas
10,000+ miles extreme road trip across
10 different countries across 8 time zones
reaching Mongolia in 40 days with
ZERO on the road backup or support.
